## üí° Insights

- Cancellation rates are strongly linked to high prices (ADR).  
- Resort Hotel shows higher cancellation ratios compared to City Hotel.  
- January has the highest cancellation rate.  
- Customers booking through **offline travel agents** tend to cancel more frequently.  
- Long lead times significantly increase the likelihood of cancellations.  
- Portugal shows noticeably higher cancellation activity.

---

## üìù Suggestions / Recommendations

1. **Optimize Pricing Strategy:**  
   Since higher prices lead to more cancellations, hotels should reduce rates during low-demand periods or offer targeted discounts.

2. **Special Offers for Resort Hotel:**  
   Weekend or holiday discounts may help reduce cancellations, especially since Resort Hotel has higher cancellation rates.

3. **Marketing Campaigns in January:**  
   Hotels should run promotions in January to counter the high cancellation volume.

4. **Improve Service Quality (especially in Portugal):**  
   Enhancing customer experience may reduce cancellations from high-cancellation regions.

---

## üîö Final Conclusion

Hotel booking cancellations are strongly influenced by pricing, seasonality, customer lead time, and booking channels. By adjusting pricing, improving regional service quality, and focusing on months with high cancellation rates, hotels can significantly reduce cancellations and increase revenue.
